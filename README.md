🍽️ NearbyBites

NearbyBites is an AI-powered restaurant recommendation chatbot that helps users discover the best dining options nearby through natural and interactive conversations. By understanding user preferences such as cuisine, budget, and location, the chatbot delivers personalized restaurant suggestions, making the search for great food faster and more convenient.

✨ Key Features
🤖 AI-Powered Chatbot – Get restaurant recommendations through natural conversations.
📍 Location-Based Search – Discover restaurants near your current location.
🍕 Cuisine Filtering – Find places based on your favorite cuisines.
💰 Budget-Friendly Suggestions – Get recommendations that match your budget.
⭐ Ratings & Reviews – View restaurant ratings to make informed decisions.
🔍 Smart Search – Quickly find restaurants based on preferences and keywords.
⚡ Real-Time Recommendations – Receive instant dining suggestions.
📱 User-Friendly Interface – Simple, responsive, and easy-to-use experience.
🎯 Personalized Results – Tailored recommendations based on user needs
